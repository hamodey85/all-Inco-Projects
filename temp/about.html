At Mr. Coffee, our engagement
begins in coffee.

We could not offer a space for freedom of expression
and sharing without thinking about what we serve in
our mugs..

All our coffees come from fair trade operations:
farmers get fair compensation for their labor, working
conditions respect the rights of the man.

This is what makes our coffees so good: they grow,
are grown, picked, and dried with respect and love.

Mr. Coffee, a coffee (almost)
like the others..

Here we come to find someone, or end up with yourself,
to work on your computer or to relax, to read among the
works of our library or just enjoy good coffee.

All this different world that meets without seeing each
other, that made you want to do something more.
This is how the philosopher Mr. Coffee was born.

Our ambition: to bring together people from all walks
of life around social issues, philosophical debates, their
shared passion for writing and letters.
